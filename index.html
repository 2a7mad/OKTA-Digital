    ABOUT US

    Who We Are ?

    Okta Digital is a digital marketing agency that provides unconventional marketing and sales services to B2C software companies.

    Our agency was founded in June 2021 and has grown into a team of 13 pioneers who desire to build the next generation of digital marketing agencies.

    Since it was founded, Okta Digital has worked with over a dozen brands owned by software companies in the United States, Europe, and Far East Asia.

    Our core team and office are located in Tripoli, Lebanon; however, we also have members working with us remotely from all around Lebanon.
